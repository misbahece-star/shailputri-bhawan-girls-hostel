Welcome Home

A residence named for strength, new beginnings and the hills that surround us

Shailputri — literally "daughter of the mountain" — is the first form of Goddess Durga, honoured on the opening day of Navratri as a symbol of new beginnings, patience and quiet strength. It is a fitting name for the hostel that houses the first home-away-from-home for the young women of Central University of Jammu, set against the Shivalik foothills of the Bagla campus.

Shailputri Bhawan brings together comfortable two- and three-seater accommodation, a dedicated study area, sports facilities, dining, and round-the-clock security, so that every resident can focus fully on her academic journey while feeling genuinely at home.

Shail — mountain, Putri — daughter. The name reflects resilience, groundedness and the promise of a fresh start — values the hostel seeks to nurture in every resident.
